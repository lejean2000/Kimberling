<h3 id="X89">X(89)&nbsp;=&nbsp;ISOGONAL CONJUGATE OF X(45)</h3>
Trilinears&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1/(2b + 2c - a) : 1/(2c + 2a - b) : 1/(2a + 2b - c)
<br>
Barycentrics&nbsp;&nbsp;a/(2b + 2c - a) : b/(2c + 2a - b) : c/(2a + 2b - c)
<p>
Let A<sub>9</sub>B<sub>9</sub>C<sub>9</sub> be the Gemini triangle 9.  Let L<sub>A</sub> be the line through A<sub>9</sub> parallel to BC, and define L<sub>B</sub> and L<sub>C</sub> cyclically.  Let A'<sub>9</sub> = L<sub>B</sub>&cap;L<sub>C</sub>, and define B'<sub>9</sub>, C'<sub>9</sub> cyclically.  Triangle A'<sub>9</sub>B'<sub>9</sub>C'<sub>9</sub> is homothetic to ABC at X(89).  (Randy Hutson, November 30, 2018)
</p>
